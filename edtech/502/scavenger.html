Copyright & Fair Use Scavenger Hunt

Learner Description: This web page contains informations for K-12 students about copyright and fair use.

What is Scavenger Hunt?

Scavenger hunt is an exercise where students answer a list of questions or solve problems as they practice information seeking skills.

What is Copyright?

Copyright is a property law. It protects the creator's stuff from being used without his/her permission.
