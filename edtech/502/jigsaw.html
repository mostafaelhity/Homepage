Instructions:

The Jigsaw activity is a group learning activity where you will learn the basic number properties. Each of you will become an expert in one of the main three number properties (associative, commutative, distributive). You will then teach other people in the class about your property .

You will begin in a home group. There will be three students in each home group. Split up into your expert group to learn your property. Your number determines which expert group you are in. Work in your expert group to learn about your assigned property. After all of the expert groups have finished then everyone returns to the home group. Each expert teaches the rest of the home group about his property.
