My name is Mostafa Elhity, I am from Alexandria, Egypt. Growing up I have always been great at Math and Science. I must have gotten it from my parents they are both teachers. Following in my parents foot steps I went to the Alexandria University. There I got a bachelors of Science and Math. Four years ago I moved to Lexington,KY. I came here to visit two of my brothers and decided to stay. I started at the bottom as a pizza driver. Working my way up I was eventually able to open my own pizza restaurant with few colleagues. After opening my business I have decided to invest in myself and go back to school. While I was searching for the right school I found Boise State University and it's amazing MET program. This website contains my projects during the program.
